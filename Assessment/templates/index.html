DATA PROTECTION IMPACT ASSESSMENT

This Toolkit is designed to simplify and implement the processing of Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA).


Instructions:

1. Please fill out the " Document Details" sheet . This sheet will contain information about the details of author and approver.

2. Please provide Inputs to all the mandatory line items which are present in "DPIA Screening " sheet. This sheet will collect         basic information about the scope of work / project and will help us understand if DPIA is required or not.

3. If 'Result' cell in "DPIA Screening" sheet shows an output as " Yes, DPIA Is Recommended " or " Yes, DPIA is          Mandatory ", then please open the "DPIA Assessment" sheet and provide your responses.

4. The concerned personnel will be requested to provide inputs to "DPIA Assessment" sheet.

5. On the basis of the repsonses provided in "DPIA Assessment" sheet , we will be preparing a "Risk Summary" Sheet.              This sheet will have the following details :
     a. Steps which are in compliance with recommended "Security by Design" prinicple of GDPR.
     b. Steps which are weak points in the current process and may be vulnerable to threats. Mitigation measures will be          mentioned with these risks.
     c. Heatmap which will depict the current scenario and possible risk at each step of the whole process.

Note 1: Data controller and Data Protection Officer should collectively analyse and provide additional checks which maybe                   required over this toolkit.
Note 2: Data Protection Officer is responsible to observe and montior if there is a possibility of any potential risk arising from               the implementation of DPIA.
